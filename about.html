About Planify

Built for people who
get things done.

Planify is an AI-powered productivity planner designed to help individuals and teams stay organized, focused, and motivated. From predictive project timelines to AI coaching, every feature is crafted with one goal: helping you achieve more with less stress.

What makes Planify different

We combine powerful productivity tools with intelligent insights to create the ultimate planning experience.

Smart Task Management

Organize tasks by priority, category, and due date. Intelligent sorting puts what matters most at the top.

Focus Timer

Built-in Pomodoro technique timer with customizable sessions to maximize deep work and scheduled breaks.

AI Productivity Insights

Get personalized productivity tips powered by AI. Contextual advice to optimize your workflow and habits.

Progress Analytics

Visual progress tracking with streak counters, completion rates, and daily performance snapshots.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Power-user keyboard shortcuts for lightning-fast task management without touching the mouse.

Works Everywhere

No downloads required. Responsive design works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Enterprise AI

Intelligent features that go beyond task lists

Planify leverages AI to give you predictive insights, scenario planning, OKR alignment, KPI dashboards, and a knowledge graph — tools that enterprises rely on.

Predictive Project Completion Dates

Our AI analyzes your velocity, task complexity, and historical patterns to predict when projects will actually finish — not just when they're planned to finish.

  • Velocity-based timeline forecasting
  • Confidence intervals (best / likely / worst case)
  • Auto-adjusts as progress changes

Scenario Simulation

Ask “What if we delay this by 2 weeks?” and see the ripple effects across your entire project timeline instantly. Model decisions before you commit.

  • “What if” timeline modeling
  • Dependency chain impact analysis
  • Compare multiple scenario branches side-by-side

AI Coaching for Managers

Get real-time coaching on workload distribution, team burnout risk, and optimal task delegation. Like having a management consultant built into your planner.

  • Workload balance recommendations
  • Burnout & overload early-warning alerts
  • Delegation suggestions based on team strengths

Knowledge Graph of Company Work

Planify maps relationships between tasks, people, projects, and decisions to build a living knowledge graph. Discover hidden dependencies and institutional knowledge at a glance.

  • Auto-mapped task & project relationships
  • Visual dependency graph explorer
  • Searchable institutional knowledge base

Company-Level OKRs Linked to Tasks

Define Objectives and Key Results at the company, team, and individual level — then link every task directly to the goals they move forward.

  • Cascading OKRs: company → team → individual
  • Every task maps to a Key Result automatically
  • Real-time OKR progress rollups across the org

Progress Forecasting

Go beyond historical tracking. Planify's AI projects future completion rates, identifies bottlenecks before they happen, and flags goals at risk of falling behind.

  • Trend-line projections for every objective
  • Bottleneck detection & early risk warnings
  • Weekly AI-generated progress summaries

AI Suggestions to Improve Goal Achievement

Planify analyzes patterns across your tasks and goals, then surfaces concrete actions — reprioritize this, delegate that, break this down — to keep you on track.

  • Smart reprioritization recommendations
  • Task decomposition suggestions for stuck goals
  • Resource reallocation nudges based on capacity

KPI Tracking Dashboards

Customizable executive dashboards that surface the metrics that matter — task throughput, cycle time, goal attainment, team velocity — all updating in real time.

  • Drag-and-drop dashboard builder
  • Pre-built templates for engineering, product & ops
  • Shareable reports with scheduled email digests
